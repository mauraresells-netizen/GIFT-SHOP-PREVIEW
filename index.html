Our Story

Gathered in Glasgow, with care.

Our wee Gift Shop began on a rainy afternoon in 2011, when Eilidh swapped a desk in the city centre for a narrow shopfront on Cresswell Lane. The idea was simple: a shop where you could find a gift you'd be genuinely proud to give — nothing mass-made, nothing forgettable.

Fourteen years on, we work with more than two hundred makers, most of them within a few miles of the front door. Every piece is chosen by hand, wrapped by hand, and sent off with a wee note if you'd like one. Come in out of the rain and have a look.
